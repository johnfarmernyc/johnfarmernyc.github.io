TEST YOUR PRODUCT BEFORE YOU BUILD IT.

With a focus on fast learning through rapid prototyping and user testing, our process gives teams a shortcut to answering critical business questions without building and launching.

We help teams move faster.

SANDBOX gives product teams the ability to design and test almost any idea in one week. Uncover user insights with a time-tested process called a design sprint, that combines ideation, rapid prototyping, and user testing.

This versatile process is perfect for building new applications, adding features to existing products with millions of users, evaluating new business opportunities, and more. 

Accelerated learning.
